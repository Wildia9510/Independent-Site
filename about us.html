Cosmetology is the practice of creating Physical beauty, and cosmetologist provides Hair, Nails, Skin Care services to clients. Hair care services include Cutting, Shampooing, Perming, Styling and Coloring. Cosmetologist also provide Makeup applications, Makeovers and Color Recommendations. Many general Cosmetologists perform skin and nail services in addition to hair care. Their Average Wage nationwide was $12.72 per hour, or $26,460 a year, according to the BLS.
